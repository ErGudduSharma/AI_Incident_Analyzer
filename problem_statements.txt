FinPay Solutions is a fintech company handling online payments.

The company runs multiple backend services like payment-service and cache systems.

When a production issue occurs, engineers receive raw and unstructured log files.

Manually analyzing these logs takes too much time.

Identifying root cause and severity depends on experience and guesswork.

There is no automatic system to suggest fixes.

Past incidents are not stored properly for future reference.

This leads to slow incident resolution and service downtime.

The company needs an AI-based system that can automatically analyze incident logs, identify the problem, assess severity, suggest fixes, and store the results for audit purposes.
